BitMal is a social market place where every one can contribute to the community.

Started in 2014 as an MIT Media Lab Whitepaper by Redha AlHaidar, BitMal evolved in 2017 to benefit from the crypto currency and blockchain exposure.

In 2018, BitMal was selected form XX proposals, winning the World Bank XX Competition.

Backed by the support of the World Bank Blockchain Lab, our first release due is October 2018, is built with the BitMal Coin (BMC), a token developed on top of Etherium.

If you would like to have a coffee and discuss BitMal, team@bitmal.org
